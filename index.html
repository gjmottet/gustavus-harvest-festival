Welcome to My Alaskan World

I'm Geneva, an Alaskan artist creating wildlife-inspired textile art in Fairbanks and Gustavus. Each piece is handcrafted with attention to detail, from halibut hot pads to beaded fingerless gloves. Alongside my fiber art, I raise happy, healthy rabbits here in the Last Frontier.

✦ Our Northern Ethos ✦
"Geneva's Trading Post turns rabbit manure into garden gold and rabbit pelts into heirlooms — and brings garden starts from our greenhouse to yours. We are a diverse, experimental micro-farm that believes in sharing what we learn to make gardening in Gustavus better for all."

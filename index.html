🏆 OFFICIAL JUDGING & AWARD STRUCTURE

Sticker Awards (per exhibit):
🟦 Blue Sticker = 90–100% of possible points — Superior
🟥 Red Sticker = 80–89% — Excellent
⬜ White Sticker = 70–79% — Good
🟢 Green Sticker = below 70% — Participation

Class Ribbons: Highest score in each class earns First Place ribbon. Second and Third place ribbons awarded at judges' discretion based on quality.

🏅 ROSETTE AWARDS (all rosettes):
🐇 Grand Champion Rabbit Rosette — highest combined total points from Rabbit (Class 1) + Cage Decor (Class 8, if entered with that rabbit) + Tanned Rabbit Skin (Class 9).
🐇 Reserve Champion Rabbit Rosette — second highest combined total points from Rabbit + Cage Decor + Rabbit Skin.
🐔 Grand Champion Chicken Rosette — highest combined total points from Chicken (Class 3) + Eggs (Class 7) + Cage Decor (Class 8, if paired with that chicken).
🐔 Reserve Champion Chicken Rosette — second highest combined total points from Chicken + Eggs + Cage Decor.
🌟 Best in Show Rosette — awarded to the exhibitor with the highest overall cumulative points across ALL their entries (any department).

Note: Cage Decor can be entered once per animal — points apply toward respective species Grand Champion and Reserve Champion calculations.
